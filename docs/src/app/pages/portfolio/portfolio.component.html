About Me

Hello, I'm Alex Rivers

I'm a professional photographer based in San Francisco with over a decade of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey began with a borrowed camera and an insatiable curiosity about how light shapes our world.

Today, I specialize in portrait photography, landscape work, and event coverage. My approach combines technical precision with an artistic eye, always striving to tell authentic stories through my images.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking the California coast, teaching photography workshops, or experimenting with new editing techniques.
